How it works

From a swipe to a start date.

No applications, no inbox black holes. Just a clear path from interest to working together.

01

Swipe

Candidates browse roles; companies browse talent. One gesture says "I'm interested" — the rest stays out of your way.

02

Match

When interest is mutual, it's a match. Both sides opted in, so every conversation starts on equal footing.

03

Chat & schedule

Message in real time, share details, and lock in an interview slot — all inside the app.

04

Start working

Send the offer, accept, and kick off the contract. Short-term work, sorted in days, not weeks.

Who it's for

Two sides, one match.

For talent

  • Find short-term gigs that fit your skills and rate
  • Only talk to companies who already want you
  • Set your availability and let work come to you

For companies

  • Fill contract roles in days with pre-interested talent
  • Swipe through candidates matched to each role
  • Chat, interview, and make offers in one place
